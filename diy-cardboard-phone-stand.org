* Overview
Sturdy, inexpensive overhead copy-stand style mount made from ordinary shipping-box cardboard. Optimized for the Samsung Galaxy A10e (147.3 × 69.6 × 8.4 mm) and batch photography of small gaming miniatures (~200 photos). Provides stable top-down or angled shots without obstructing legs.

**Why this design?** Wide weighted base + reinforced post and arm for zero wobble during repeated use. Completely free if you have old boxes.

* Materials
- 2–3 corrugated cardboard shipping boxes (thicker/double-wall preferred)
- Packing tape or duct tape (generous amount)
- Optional: hot glue or white glue
- Tools: utility knife / scissors, ruler, pencil

* Dimensions (Recommended)
- Base: 18" × 18" (45 cm × 45 cm)
- Vertical post height: 15–18" (38–46 cm)
- Horizontal arm reach: 8–10" (20–25 cm) over subject
- Phone cradle: fits A10e snugly in landscape orientation

* Step-by-Step Build Instructions

**1. Super-Stable Base**
- Cut two 18" × 18" squares.
- Stack and fully tape/glue together into one thick slab for weight and rigidity.

**2. Vertical Post**
- Cut four 4" × 18" strips.
- Stack/layer (or roll into tube first) and tape/glue into a thick, rigid beam.

**3. Attach Post to Base**
- Position post upright in center of base.
- Add two triangular braces (~6" × 8") on both sides.
- Tape/glue heavily and wrap joints with multiple layers of tape.

**4. Horizontal Arm (Boom)**
- Cut another 4"-wide × 12" layered beam.
- Attach at right angle (or slight downward tilt) to top of post.
- Reinforce underside with triangular braces + heavy taping.

**5. Phone Cradle (Galaxy A10e Specific)**
- Cut 5" × 8" double-layered rectangle.
- Cut central slot: 9 mm (⅜") wide × 70 mm (2¾") long.
- Add small side lips/tabs to prevent sliding.
- Attach cradle to end of arm so camera points down.

**6. Final Reinforcement**
- Cover all joints with 3–4 layers of tape.
- Optional: run hot glue along inside corners.
- Let dry if glued.

* Usage Tips for Miniatures Photography
- Place miniature under arm.
- Slide phone into cradle (use timer or Bluetooth shutter to avoid touching stand).
- Adjust angle/height with small risers under miniatures if needed.
- Store assembled or detach arm for flat storage.

* Tips for Maximum Sturdiness
- Use thickest corrugated cardboard available.
- Add extra braces/tape if any flex appears.
- Optional: spray paint black for professional look.
