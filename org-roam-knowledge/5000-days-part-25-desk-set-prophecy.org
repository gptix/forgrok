* 5000 Days Part 25: The Desk Set Prophecy

1957 called.

In the long arc of our collective story, certain artifacts from the past arrive like messages in bottles... *Desk Set* (1957) is one such relic, a shimmering, color-saturated romantic comedy that delivers a precise blueprint of the tensions now unfolding in the Interregnum.

This film perfectly predicted our modern anxieties about AI — corporate spin, system meltdowns, AI hallucinations, and the Productivity Paradox — decades before those terms existed.
