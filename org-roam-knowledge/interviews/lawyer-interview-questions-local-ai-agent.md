# Proposed 30-Minute Interview Questions for Lawyers

**Focus**: How a **local / private** AI agent could help their practice.

### Suggested Questions
1. Background & current tools
2. Most repetitive/time-consuming tasks
3. Document drafting & review bottlenecks
4. Legal research frustrations
5. Client intake & knowledge reuse
6. Administrative/client communication pain points
7. Value of a **fully local** AI agent (no cloud)
8. Privacy / attorney-client privilege concerns
9. Biggest potential impact if available tomorrow
10. Adoption barriers (ethical, technical, cost)

**Emphasis**: Highlight local/offline nature for confidentiality.
