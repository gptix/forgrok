* Precis
Roemmele describes the profound psychological and existential crisis — the “Dark Night of the Soul” — that individuals and societies will face as AI automation strips away traditional sources of identity and purpose.

* Summary
In Part 6, Roemmele explores the inevitable period of disorientation, grief, and loss of meaning when work — long the primary source of identity, status, and purpose — is largely automated away. This collective “Dark Night of the Soul” is not a failure but a necessary passage. The essay offers guidance on how to navigate this dark night consciously, emerging with a new, more resilient sense of meaning and purpose in the age of abundance.
