* Precis
Roemmele introduces the “Hidden Sacristy Architect” — the quiet, deliberate builder who creates and protects the sacred spaces of human wisdom and meaning in the coming age of abundance.

* Summary
In Part 15, Roemmele shifts focus to the quiet, behind-the-scenes work of preserving what makes us human. The “Hidden Sacristy Architect” is the person (or community) who intentionally builds and guards the invisible repositories — the “sacristies” — of deep human knowledge, values, and creativity. These architects work outside the spotlight to ensure that wisdom, craft, and spiritual insight survive automation and remain accessible to future generations.
