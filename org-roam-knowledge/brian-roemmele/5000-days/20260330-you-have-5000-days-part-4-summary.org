* Precis
Roemmele shifts from warning to empowerment: the coming AI abundance is not inevitable doom but a historic opportunity that must be actively reframed and shaped during the critical 5,000-day window.

* Summary
In Part 4, Roemmele moves beyond the diagnosis of deskilling and introduces the positive reframing required for the post-labor era. He argues that material abundance created by AI is not a threat to be feared but a dawn that humanity must consciously shape. The essay explores how individuals can replace the old scarcity-based identity with new sources of meaning, creativity, and contribution. Key ideas include moving from passive consumption to active creation, building new personal “operating systems,” and treating the next 5,000 days as the last chance to design a humane and purposeful post-work society.
