* Precis
Roemmele presents the hopeful counterpart to deskilling: the rebirth of the artisan — humans who create, craft, and innovate purely for the joy of creation once AI removes economic necessity.

* Summary
Part 9 describes the cultural and personal renaissance that becomes possible in the age of AI abundance. With material needs met by automation, humans can return to the artisan mindset — mastery for its own sake, creativity without the pressure of survival, and the deep satisfaction of making things with care and intention. Roemmele argues that this “Artisan’s Awakening” is not a luxury but the natural next stage of human development if we consciously prepare for it during the 5,000-day window.
