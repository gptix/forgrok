* Precis
Roemmele describes the practical emergence of a new artisan economy in which individuals use AI as a powerful partner to create value, meaning, and income with minimal capital and labor.

* Summary
In Part 24, Roemmele paints a concrete, optimistic picture of daily life in the abundance era. Traditional jobs disappear, but a vibrant new artisan economy rises in their place. Individuals become owners of small, high-leverage AI-augmented enterprises — rural cybercab fleets, creative studios, wisdom-preservation projects, local services — all powered by the Dynamic Duo partnership. The 5,000-day window is the time to begin building these artisan businesses and identities so that when the old economy fully transitions, we are already thriving in the new one.
