* Precis
Roemmele uses John Calhoun’s famous “Universe 25” mouse utopia experiment as a stark warning of what happens when material abundance removes all struggle without a higher purpose.

* Summary
In Part 16, Roemmele presents the chilling results of Calhoun’s Universe 25 experiment: when mice were given unlimited food, space, and safety, their society rapidly collapsed into violence, withdrawal, and extinction. He draws a direct parallel to humanity’s coming AI-driven abundance: without deliberate new sources of meaning and purpose, material plenty can lead to the same behavioral sink. The 5,000-day window is our last chance to design a human “Universe 25” that avoids the mouse-utopia trap.
