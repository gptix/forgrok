* Precis
Roemmele describes the chaotic “interregnum” period between the old world of work and the new world of abundance, and how individuals can navigate it as heroes on a deliberate path forward.

* Summary
In Part 13, Roemmele frames the next 5,000 days as an interregnum — a time of turbulence, uncertainty, and institutional breakdown between the ending of the old scarcity/work-based order and the emergence of a stable post-labor civilization. He presents this chaos not as something to fear but as the necessary arena in which each person can choose the hero’s path: conscious action, wisdom preservation, and active design of the future rather than passive drifting.
