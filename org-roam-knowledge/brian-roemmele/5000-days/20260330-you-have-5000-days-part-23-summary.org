* Precis
Roemmele brings the series to its philosophical climax: humanity now faces a binary civilizational choice between two futures — one of passive consumption and one of active creation.

* Summary
In Part 23, Roemmele distills the entire 22 previous essays into one decisive question: will we allow AI abundance to turn us into passive, over-protected consumers (the “With Folded Hands” / “Universe 25” outcome), or will we choose to become active artisans, architects, and dynamic-duo partners who shape a flourishing post-labor civilization? The 5,000-day window is the last moment in which that choice is still fully ours to make.
