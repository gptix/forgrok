* Precis
Roemmele brings the series to its climax: the next 5,000 days are not just a transition — they are the final, decisive period in which humanity chooses its destiny in the age of AI abundance.

* Summary
In Part 22, Roemmele zooms out to the big picture. The 5,000-day window is the last realistic time in which we can still shape the outcome of the automation revolution. Every choice made now — personal, cultural, institutional — will determine whether we end up in a benevolent abundance or a new form of dystopia. He calls for urgent, collective, and individual action: preserve wisdom, build the Dynamic Duo relationship, become the Artisan and the Architect, and consciously design the post-labor civilization before the window closes.
