* Precis
Roemmele presents a concrete, optimistic vision: in the age of abundance you can own and operate a small fleet of autonomous cybercab vehicles from a rural location, creating meaningful income and purpose with almost no overhead.

* Summary
In Part 18, Roemmele offers one of the most practical and empowering examples in the series. He shows how AI-enabled autonomous vehicles (cybercabs) will allow ordinary individuals to own and run a small transportation business from anywhere — even a rural property — with minimal capital and labor. This “Rural Cybercab Company” becomes a model for the new artisan economy: using advanced technology to create real value while retaining full human agency and creativity. The essay demonstrates that abundance does not have to mean idleness; it can mean owning and operating small, high-leverage enterprises that serve real human needs.
