* Precis
Roemmele draws a parallel between the original Gilded Age of extreme inequality and the new Gilded Age that AI abundance could create unless we consciously design a more equitable outcome.

* Summary
In Part 12, Roemmele warns that the coming wave of AI-driven wealth creation risks repeating the worst features of the historical Gilded Age: massive concentration of riches in the hands of a tiny few while the majority are left deskilled and economically sidelined. He argues that the 5,000-day window is our last realistic chance to avoid a new Gilded Age and instead build a truly abundant society where the benefits of automation are broadly shared.
