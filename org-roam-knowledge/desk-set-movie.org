#+title: The Desk Set Movie (1957)
#+filetags: :roemmele:desk-set:film-prophecy:
#+date: 2026-04-22

The film unfolds at the Federal Broadcasting Network in midtown Manhattan. Bunny Watson (Katharine Hepburn) leads a lively team of reference librarians. Spencer Tracy’s Richard Sumner arrives with EMERAC, the room-sized “electronic brain”.

The movie dramatizes the exact tensions we face today: automation anxiety, corporate promises of “freeing workers from drudgery,” and the reality that humans remain irreplaceable.
