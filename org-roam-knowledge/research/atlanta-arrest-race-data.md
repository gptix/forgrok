# Research Goal

Quantify fraction of arrestees / indictees in Atlanta (Fulton County) / Georgia with inaccurate race recorded in police and court documents.

Inspired by Matt van Swol’s North Carolina work.

### Data Sources
- Fulton County Sheriff Inmate Search (public mugshots + recorded race)
- Fulton County Clerk of Superior Court
- Georgia Open Records Act requests

**National Context** (NBER 2024): ~17% mismatch rate for defendants, mostly Hispanics recorded as White.

**Next Steps**: Sample recent bookings, compare recorded race vs. mugshot + name.
