# Real Estate Lawyers Using AI in 2026

Adoption rate ~**61%** (ABA Legal Technology Survey), up sharply from 2024.

### Primary Use Cases
- **Contract review & redlining** (purchase agreements, leases, loan docs)
- **Lease abstraction** (extracting key terms from commercial leases)
- **Due diligence & title analysis**
- **Document generation** (closing checklists, disclosures, client letters)
- **Legal research** (zoning, easements, regulatory updates)

**Highest ROI** in commercial real estate transactions.

### Tools Commonly Used
- Spellbook, LegalOn, Harvey, Claude
- Kira Systems, Diligen for abstraction
- Clio Duo, Lexis+ Protégé for workflows

**Key Insight**: Lawyers treat AI as a highly capable junior associate — excellent at first-pass document work and research, but final judgment, negotiation strategy, and client counseling remain human.
